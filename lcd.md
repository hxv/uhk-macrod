# `lcd`
Displays my animated nick on an LCD display.

```
setLedTxt 150
setLedTxt 150 !!-
setLedTxt 150 !--
setLedTxt 150 ---
setLedTxt 150 --
setLedTxt 150 -

setLedTxt 150 /
setLedTxt 150 |
setLedTxt 150 \
setLedTxt 150 -
setLedTxt 150 H

setLedTxt 150 H-
setLedTxt 150 H/
setLedTxt 150 H|
setLedTxt 150 H\
setLedTxt 150 H-
setLedTxt 150 H/
setLedTxt 150 HX

setLedTxt 150 HX-
setLedTxt 150 HX/
setLedTxt 150 HX|

setLedTxt 150 HX/
setLedTxt 150 HXV
```
